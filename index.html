About Me

Hey There! First of all, I love to read and I'm a learner, not a person to be confined within the four walls of room. I am from a small town in Bangladesh called Habiganj and I grew up in a very lovely family with my one and only sister and my parents.
I'm a student and an artist. Specially love to draw cartoons. One more thing! I love to imagine so much. I write what I imagine and sometimes those turn into poetries! I love to make songs and sing loudly like a free bird. And two more things i love to do! I am very fond of gaming and designing webs also.
